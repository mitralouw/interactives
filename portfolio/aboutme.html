About Me

I'm a third-year student at Northwestern University looking to pursue journalism, data science and higher education. I've previously worked at the Pittsburgh Business Times, TABLE Magazine, PublicSource and Injustice Watch and am a senior features editor for North by Northwestern, our student magazine. Academically, I specialize in Central Asian and Middle Eastern politics, history and international relations. I always have a podcast going (current favorite is Radio Atlantic) and in my ever-shrinking free time I love to run and attempt to cook elaborate dinners.
